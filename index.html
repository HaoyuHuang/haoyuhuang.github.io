Biography

I am a software enginner at Google, working on query processing and autonomous database in AlloyDB

I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Southern California in 2020 under the supervision of Shahram Ghandeharizadeh. My Ph.D. dissertation is Component-based Distributed Data Stores.

My research interests include database kernel, caching, and distributed database.
